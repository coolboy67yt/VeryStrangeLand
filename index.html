About Very Strange Land

Welcome to Very Strange Land, a theme park like no other! Brace yourself for a thrilling experience with attractions like Lime's Drop, Cyan's Castle, LuLu Land, Grayscale Racers, Yellow's Rollercoaster, Pink's Maze, Haunted House, Cyan's Wheel, Capitan Red's Restaurant, and the Draincade. Our mascots, Red the Captain, Yellow the miner, Cyan the king, Lime the banjo-player, Pink the wizard, and Gray the biker, are here to make your day unforgettable. Relive the 90's nostalgia and create memories with your friends and family!
